Instant Apps with VS Online, Git, and Azure

Wednesday
2:30 PM – 3:45 PM
Meeting Room 16/17

Recent enhancements to Visual Studio Online, including Git support and deployment to Azure, enable developers to forget about infrastructure or ops, and dive straight into delivering value - all for free. In this session, we'll take a look at how a real world project can go from File > New Project to a continuously deployed minimum viable product just about as fast as you can code it, and continue to grow from there.

Agile Azure Continuous Deployment Git Intermediate Visual Studio Online

David Ruttka

David Ruttka has been "making computers do things" since he first saw King's Quest on a 286 PC in the mid-80's, but he turned it into a career in the early-00's. The majority of his experience is on the Microsoft stack, primarily using C#, the .NET Framework, and JavaScript. When he isn't coding, David enjoys spending time with his family, watching hockey, reading, and playing the occasional video game. David can be found on Twitter @druttka and on his development blog at davidruttka.com.
