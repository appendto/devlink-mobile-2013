Customizing nopCommerce with Plugins & Themes

Thursday
9:30 AM – 10:45 AM
Meeting Room 4

This talk picks up where we left off with Building MVC eCommerce Sites and shows how nopCommerce can be easily customized using plugins and themes without having to modify the original source code. This approach allows for quick and easy upgrades as new versions of nopCommerce are released. A list of past and scheduled speaking engagements can be found here

C# Entity Framework LINQ MVC Model-View-Controller Plugins Themes WCF Advanced ECommerce NopCommerce

Gaines Kergosien

Gaines Kergosien is a senior solutions architect with Vanick Digital and serves as a board member for the Nashville .NET User Group, the INETA Mentor for TN and speaks at software development conferences throughout the United States. With over 12 years in solutions development using Microsoft technologies, his work includes consulting for such companies as Deloitte, Hospital Corporation of America (HCA), American General (AIG), Lexis Nexis, Gibson Guitars, and Cardinal Healthcare.
