Steal All The Good Ideas

Thursday
4:00 PM – 5:15 PM
Meeting Room 4

You've probably seen good ideas like actors, functional concurrent programming models, and fault-tolerance show up in your favorite languages over the past few years. Come join us to learn about Erlang, a language with all these features plus more. In this session we will begin with an introduction to Erlang, talk about where to use/not use it, and how to apply some of it's ideas to other languages. This session is for developers interested in bending their minds bit for functional concurrent programming.

Concurrency Erlang Functional Programming Beginner

Alex Moore

Alex Moore is a Client Services Engineer at Basho Technologies, where he helps customers and community members solve interesting NoSql and Distributed Systems problems. Formerly a .Net Web Consultant, Alex now spends his days frolicking with functional languages.
