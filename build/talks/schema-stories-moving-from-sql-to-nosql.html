Schema Stories - Moving from SQL to NoSQL

Thursday
8:00 AM – 9:15 AM
Meeting Room 12

These kids nowadays, trying to throw away 30 years of RDBMS knowledge for what? Availability and partition tolerance for one thing, blistering speed for another. The only question left on your mind is "Do I denormalize everything, or just some things?". In this session, we will cover how and where to denormalize data, how to optimize for reads vs writes, and how to optimize your search experience with Term-based Inverted Indexes using Riak, a Key-Value database. This session is for senior developers and DBAs that are interested in looking into the other side.

NoSQL Riak SQL Beginner

Alex Moore

Alex Moore is a Client Services Engineer at Basho Technologies, where he helps customers and community members solve interesting NoSql and Distributed Systems problems. Formerly a .Net Web Consultant, Alex now spends his days frolicking with functional languages.
