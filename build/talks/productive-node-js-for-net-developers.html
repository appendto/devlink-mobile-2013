Productive Node.js For .NET Developers

So you're a seasoned .NET dev... you've been around the C# block a time or two. You know your delegates from your MVC controllers from your XAML. You know JavaScript pretty well too, seen your share of JQuery... and you've perhaps built some modern web apps using Angular or Bootstrap. And then somehow, you find yourself in the strange world of Node. What. Is. This. ? This talk is geared toward .NET developers with basic Node familiarity. We'll do a quick "Hello, world" for the newbs and then dive straight into patterns, libraries, and tools to make you more productive and comfortable in the Node ecosystem. We'll also discuss ways to bridge the worlds of Node and .NET so that you can leverage code you've already written. You'll walk out with practical guidance you can put to immediate use on your next (first?) Node project.

.NET C# Node Intermediate

Josh Lane

I'm a Senior Architect for Wintellect in Atlanta, GA. I specialize in architecture, design, implementation, and troubleshooting of enterprise systems in .NET, Node, and Azure. My dog barks too much.
