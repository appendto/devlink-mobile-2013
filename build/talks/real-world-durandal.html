Real-World Durandal

Friday
1:00 PM – 2:15 PM
Meeting Room 9/10

After having deployed multiple sites/projects based on DurandalJS, it's time to share lessons learned, talk about how to make Durandal sing, and some traps to avoid. We'll look at actual real code rather than just the typical Starter Kit stuff, and focus on how you can get started quickly, and publish well.

Durandal Intermediate Javascript Knockout

Jason Clark

Jason was a Senior Software Consultant for Avatar Software in Brentwood, TN and is now a Senior Software Developer for Tresta in Tallahassee, FL. He loves working in JavaScript, especially with Durandal, Knockout and Sails.js. He has completed 5 production Durandal sites, from Durandal 1.2 - 2.1, has presented on Durandal to the Nashville .NET User Group and is looking forward to meeting Rob Eisenberg.
