Powering Your Apps with Live Services!

Thursday
2:30 PM – 3:45 PM
Meeting Room 16/17

Guess how many people use Live Services? 500 million plus! Every time you use Live/Hotmail, Calendar or SkyDrive you are leveraging the cloud through MSFT’s Live Services. They are ubiquitous and have made their way into our smartphones/tablets through integration of services. And when your applications connect to the same services, they start feeling right at home with what users are already used to. Enter Live Connect APIs – easy RESTful hooks to leverage Live Services, along with SDKs for 3rd party applications on desktop/web/mobile platforms. How could you integrate Live Services in your Web apps? The Windows platform support is given, but what about going cross platform? Manage data, authentication, authorization & services in one swoop. Come join us as we explore some of these new opportunities through demos & code samples. Leave the talk knowing you don't always have to pay for the cloud; rather use what your consumer already knows and supports multiple platforms. Live Services FTW!
