Javascriptisms: 7 Things You Must Know About JS

JavaScript is all the rage right now, and many of us are now using JS on a regular basis in our jobs. But do you really feel like you know the language? Or does working in JavaScript still feel a bit like trying to communicate in a foreign country? Join me as I cover 7 core areas of the JavaScript language that are often the biggest pain points. Knowing them is guaranteed to help you feel more at home writing JS code. - Type Coercion and Logical Expressions - Scope and Closures - Hoisting - Objects, Prototype and the `new` keyword - for...in - The `this` pointer with Call, Apply and Bind - Promises
