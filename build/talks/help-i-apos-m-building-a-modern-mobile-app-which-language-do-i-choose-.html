Help! I'm Building a Modern Mobile App. Which Language Do I Choose?

Thursday
8:00 AM – 9:15 AM
Meeting Room F

Just getting started in Modern app development? Not really sure where to start or even what is available to use? Between Windows, iOS, and Android it can be daunting wondering how to approach app building but look no further. In this session, we will discuss what your language options are, based on a variety of real world scenarios and constraints that you are likely to encounter on your way toward publishing a modern app in one or more marketplaces. In addition, I will cover things you need to consider such as the app type, developer experience, or whether you are migrating this app from an existing platform as well as what cross platform considerations you’ll need.

C# HTML JavaScript Languages Mobile Mobile Web Modern Apps XAML General Audience
