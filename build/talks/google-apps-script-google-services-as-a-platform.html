Google Apps Script: Google Services as a Platform

Thursday
8:00 AM – 9:15 AM
Meeting Room E

One of the distinct advantages the Google platform is the rich variety of services offered. Unfortunately, accessing and integrating those services can be a challenge. Google Apps Script is a platform for building web applications using Google services. Using JavaScript as the language of choice, Google Apps Script combines many of Google’s popular services including Docs, Spreadsheets, Calendar and Mail with a browser-based IDE and debugger. Add in a rich library of utility services and you can develop a complete application without ever leaving the browser!

Douglas Starnes

Douglas Starnes is a polyglot ninja in the Memphis, TN area. He has spent the past 7 years developing software in multiple languages including C#, Python, JavaScript and more on mobile, cloud and web platforms. His community involvement includes speaking at conferences such as DevLink, St. Louis Days of .NET, CodePaLOUsa, CodeStock and PyOhio in addition to the .NET and Java User Groups in Memphis. He is the co-organizer of the Memphis Python User Group (MEMPy) and one of the organizers of PyTennessee, the annual state of Tennessee Python conference. Douglas is also a founding board member of GeekMemphis, the local technology advisory organization in Memphis.
