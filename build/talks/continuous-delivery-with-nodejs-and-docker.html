Continuous Delivery With NodeJS and Docker

Friday
8:00 AM – 9:15 AM
Meeting Room F

Have you ever been frustrated by how difficult it is to introduce a new service or technology to your stack? Have you ever been burned by complex deployment processes that lead to failing code? Do you often catch yourself saying, “But it worked on my machine!”? Docker uses a Linux Kernel feature that allows you to create self-contained applications and services and ship them between environments in a secure and reliable way. Come learn how you can use Docker and NodeJS to build light-weight, resilient, self-updating containers that will stream-line and simplify how your code goes from development to production.

Continuous-Delivery DevOps Docker NodeJS Advanced

Alex Robson

Alex is a systems architect and developer who enjoys working on distributed systems where performance and scale matter.
