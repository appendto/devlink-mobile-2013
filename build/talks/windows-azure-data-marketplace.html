Windows Azure Data Marketplace

The Windows Azure Data Marketplace is a little known service that provides currently 162 data sets easily accessible with Microsoft Excel or any application capable of consuming an OData service. Best of all, over 100 of these data sets are free. All that is needed is a free Microsoft Live ID and 10 minutes of your time to get started. No credit card required. In this talk, I will introduce the Data Marketplace and the wide variety of data available, demonstrate pulling the data into Excel, show how to consume the OData service with just 3 lines of C#.NET code. I will wrap up the talk showing how easy it is to publish your own data set in the Data Marketplace and charge a monthly subscription fee to quickly earn revenue from over 88 countries worldwide. Microsoft handles all the revenue transactions, all you have to do is enjoy your monthly revenue check!
