Introduction to SQL Server Policy-Based Management

We'll go beyond the simple examples for keeping your database clean and manageable by exploring many of the amazingly rich facets and conditions that you can control automatically with SQL Server's built-in policy-based management tools. We'll look at the proactive and reactive approaches to policing that will work best in your shop to keep your database developers on the right track. During this highly-interactive session, the presenter will let you guide the discussion and the examples with your questions and concerns. Please join us to learn about this most excellent yet mostly-unknown feature set in SQL Server.

Management Policy SQL Beginner

Kevin Hazzard

Kevin Hazzard is a long-time C# MVP who has been serving his clients as a database architect for even longer. He's the author of Metaprogramming in .NET by Manning Press and a Group Principal at Linchpin People, leaders in the SQL Server database wellness and data integration spaces.
