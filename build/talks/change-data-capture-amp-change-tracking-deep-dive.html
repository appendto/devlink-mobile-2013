Change Data Capture & Change Tracking Deep Dive

Change Data Capture (CDC) and Change Tracking (CT) have been features in SQL Server for several years now but there's not really that much useful information in the blogosphere about how they work under the hood. Understanding when you should select one or the other also isn't widely known. We will dig deep into the Microsoft implementations of CDC and CT, showing you through several real-world examples how to make these great features serve you and your organization well. This will be a highly-interactive, highly-entertaining session with lots of questions answered and mysteries revealed.

Auditing Change Data Capture Change Tracking SQL Intermediate

Kevin Hazzard

Kevin Hazzard is a long-time C# MVP who has been serving his clients as a database architect for even longer. He's the author of Metaprogramming in .NET by Manning Press and a Group Principal at Linchpin People, leaders in the SQL Server database wellness and data integration spaces.
