Stepping Up – Taking the leap into technical leadership

Wednesday
2:30 PM – 3:45 PM
Meeting Room 7/8

There are lots of sessions on software development tips and tricks and many of the developers here can benefit from those greatly, but there is a time when most (not all) developers need to step-up and take the role of becoming a leader in their organization or group. Doing that requires you to relinquish control in some areas and take control of others. This presentation is help developers that are ready, or are getting ready, to take that next step into technical leadership. Here you’ll learn about vision, influence, and how to be a good leader. This gives you the building blocks of what you need to know about leading a team. We’ll get rid of some leadership myths and talk about managing change, projects, and people in a practical way that will give new leaders a few things that they can start doing today.

Leadership Communication General Audience Influence Management Trust Vision

John Rouda
